## 🎯 **Benefits of New Structure**

### **1. Executive Summary First**
- ✅ **Quick overview**: Summary statistics at the top
- ✅ **Category breakdown**: See distribution of sensitive content types
- ✅ **Key metrics**: Total names, classifications, segments, pages

### **2. Better for Stakeholders**
- ✅ **Management review**: Can quickly see document overview
- ✅ **Compliance check**: See total sensitive content count
- ✅ **Risk assessment**: Category breakdown shows what types of sensitive data

### **3. Professional Format**
- ✅ **Business report style**: Summary first, details second
- ✅ **Easy scanning**: Key information prominently displayed
- ✅ **Complete picture**: Overview + detailed analysis
